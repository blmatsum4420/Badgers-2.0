The

problem

As climate change brings a new frontier of hotter summers, drier winters, and harsher air, the question of what is coming looms. Our group wanted to analyze past historical weather data in order to evaluate the potential impact on weather conditions UC Davis will face in the future. Furthermore, our team understands that the effects of climate change overreaches the boundaries of mother nature. With effects such as poverty being aggravated by the inequalities of climate change, we wanted to see if there was a correlation in Davis.
